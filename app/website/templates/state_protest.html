In compliance with regulatory standards, {{state}} now enforces age verification for accessing this website and numerous others containing adult content. While intended to shield minors from inappropriate material, this measure encroaches upon the rights of adults to access protected speech. Moreover, the efficacy and potential risks associated with requiring age identification for online adult content remain questionable.

We and many other adult content websites prioritize the protection of minors from viewing adult content, which is why many of us have Parental Controls on our websites to help parents monitor what their children view online.

We strongly believe that relying solely on the government to make laws and regulate online content, when parents are perfectly capable of doing so themselves, could actually do more harm than good. Mandating age verification without effective enforcement methods leaves platforms with the choice to comply or not. This leaves thousands of platforms vulnerable to minors who may not follow the same protective measures we take at {{site_name}}.com. Additionally, many of these platforms may lack proper parental controls, making the situation even more concerning.

Ensuring the safety of online users is paramount to us. However, the age verification law in {{state}} seems ineffective and potentially risky for users. Numerous adult content sites operate from foreign countries with varying standards for online user protection. While {{site_name}}.com is US based, many others aren't. Requiring age verification could entail providing personal ID to websites, potentially exposing sensitive data to multiple foreign entities and increasing the risk of identity-related issues.
