Use of cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content and ads, provide social media features, and analyze our traffic. By continuing to use our site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with our Cookie Policy. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

As you navigate through and interact with {{site_name}}, we use automatic data collection technologies to collect your activity on our website. The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include cookies, which are small text files that are stored in your browser or downloaded to your device when you visit {{site_name}}. What cookies do we use on {{site_name}}.

  • Necessary cookies: These cookies are required for our website. These nescessary cookies include, color theme, user login information (username, not password), or if a user has access to a certain service or has requested a certain language.
  • Targeting and Advertising Cookies: These cookies help {{site_name}} keep a record of a users interests so that we can serve content and ads based on that users interests
  • Persistent Cookies: unlike session cookies, are designed to stay on your device until they expire or are manually deleted. The expiration dates of these cookies are determined by the cookies themselves, with some expiring within minutes and others lasting for days.

Cookies play a crucial role in gathering valuable data for the benefit of our users. However, if you prefer, you have the option to disable these cookies in the site settings.

We do not use session cookies.
